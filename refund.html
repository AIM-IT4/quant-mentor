Refund Policy

2. Digital Products

  • Non-Downloaded Products: Full refund within 7 days if not downloaded
  • Downloaded Products: No refund once download link is accessed

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Send an email to jha.8@alumni.iitj.ac.in
  2. Include your order/payment ID
  3. State the reason for refund request
  4. Allow 3-5 business days for processing

4. Processing Time

  • You will receive email confirmation once processed

5. Exceptions

Refunds may not be granted in the following cases:

  • Customer's inability to attend due to technical issues on their end

6. Contact for Refunds

Response Time: Within 24-48 hours
